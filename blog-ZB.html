Fast and stable internet in Rwanda

  • Zuba Broadband

Zuba Broadband, an authorized Starlink reseller in Rwanda, recently completed a successful installation at Hotel Talents Pool in Nyamasheke. The school, located in a picturesque but remote area, had struggled with reliable internet access until Zuba stepped in. The professional installation team ensured a smooth setup, aligning the Starlink dish for optimal connectivity, and the hotel's management was thrilled with the transformation. With fast and stable internet now available, guests can enjoy seamless streaming and online services, improving the hotel’s reputation and customer experience.

Fast and stable internet now available!

Since Starlink operates using satellite signals, a clear view of the sky is essential to ensure uninterrupted connectivity. The installation team at Hotel Talents Pool carefully positioned the dish on the hotel’s rooftop, avoiding trees, buildings, and other obstacles. This unique feature makes Starlink the perfect solution for remote businesses, where laying fiber lines is challenging or impossible. With Zuba Broadband’s expert guidance, the hotel now enjoys consistent internet access without the limitations of traditional infrastructure.

Whether for hotels, offices, farms, or factories, Starlink provides reliable performance, allowing companies to focus on their goals without worrying about connectivity issues. With Zuba Broadband’s support, businesses can unlock the full potential of satellite internet and elevate their operations to new heights.
