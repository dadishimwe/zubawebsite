Connecting the Future: Zuba Broadband Headquarters

  • Dadi Ishimwe
  • 0ct 16, 2024

In a remarkable move toward enhancing connectivity, the headquarters of Zuba Broadband recently installed Starlink satellite internet. The installation was carried out by a skilled team of professional technicians, whose expertise ensured a smooth setup. From aligning the satellite dish to integrating Starlink with existing network infrastructure, every step was handled meticulously. This project marks a leap forward for Zuba Broadband in offering lightning-fast internet solutions.

The process began with the installation of the Starlink Dishy antenna on the rooftop, positioned to maintain an unobstructed view of the sky. Technicians utilized advanced tools to measure signal strength and align the dish with the optimal satellite beam. The dish was then linked to a Starlink router, ensuring seamless distribution of internet throughout the headquarters. Testing was done rigorously to verify that everything ran without a hitch—confirming both reliability and performance.

Starlink's speed at 220 Mbps download and 25 Mbps upload has lived up to the hype!

Starlink's speed at 220 Mbps download and 25 Mbps upload has lived up to the hype, revolutionizing internet access at the headquarters. Employees now experience minimal latency during video conferences and enjoy rapid download speeds for large files. Whether streaming or conducting data-heavy operations, the connection remains stable and fast. This installation demonstrates how satellite internet can be a game-changer for companies operating in both urban and remote locations.
